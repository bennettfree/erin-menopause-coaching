About Erin

Meet Erin Free
Based in Napa Valley, Erin brings a grounded, compassionate approach to helping women navigate midlife. As a mother, listener, and lifelong health advocate, her mission is to walk alongside each client with personalized care, practical tools, and deep respect for this powerful transition. Her programs are designed to support women in feeling whole, balanced, and confident in their bodies again, without the overwhelm or shame.

Our Mission

My mission is to help women feel strong, clear, and confident through menopause. Every client receives a personalized plan rooted in real life health coaching, covering everything from nutrition and movement to hormone education and mindset.

Each experience is shaped by time, attention, and care. No rushed sessions or generic advice - just thoughtful guidance based on each client’s unique story, symptoms, and goals.

This is care that meets women where they are, with real solutions, meaningful support, and lasting tools to feel like themselves again.
